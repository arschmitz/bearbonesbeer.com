43 Lisbon St Lewiston, ME

Bear Bones Beer is an independent nano-brewery located in downtown Lewiston, Maine with a focus on sustainability and quality.



We brew our beer in small batches ensuring freshness, and allowing us to be responsive to the wishes of our constituents.

Our beer is kegged or poured into reusable take out vessels and sampling glasses, right at our brewery and tasting room.



We use ‘Standard’ 750ml bottles for take out, instead of the common half gallon growler. This size is allows you to pour two glasses from each bottle, and if thats not enough we offer 4Pack discounts when you buy multiple bottles. Cub Club Members will receive stainless steel 1L bottles, filled for the same price as the standard 750!”
